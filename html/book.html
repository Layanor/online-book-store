Brain Games - Sticker Activity: There Was an Old Lady Who Swallowed the Alphabet!

By: Publications International Ltd.


 Introduce children to the alphabet in a fun, interactive way with this sticker activity book! Discover 27 activities and more than 150 stickers. Activities include matching pictures and words, finding missing letters that start the words, and adding stickers to finish the picture.
